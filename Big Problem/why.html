Why?

Good question. Pineapple is too sweet and it tastes out of place compared to other foods on the pizza. It's kind of like having a big slice of tomato on a burger. It could be a great burger, but once you get to the tomato, that's all you can taste. It's too powerful and overshadows the taste of the other ingredients.

Pineapple is too powerful and sweet to be on pizza. When you bite into a slice of pineapple pizza, you get this out of place sweet taste in your mouth that throws you off and lowers the quality of the meal.
